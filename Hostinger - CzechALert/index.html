What we can help you with

Discover how our expertise in digital investigation combined with fieldwork can protect and empower your goals.

Business Due Diligence & Risk Management

Do you have a prospective business partner, but are unsure about their reputation, or whether you can trust them? Based on your needs and objectives, we will create a structured risk report about the target using open and human sources alike. No-one will slip past your radar ever again.

Strategic Intelligence & Consulting

Do you need to make an important executive decision, but are lacking all the necessary information? This is where we step in. Based on a deep contextual and evidence-based analysis, we will outline the optimal strategy for your specific situation. Informed decision-makers represent the way of the future.

Judicial & Personal Investigations

Do you want to file a criminal report against someone, but are unsure of how to do it or lack the necessary evidence? Have you recently met someone very charming, but something seems slightly off? We will happily look into such cases for you and help you take the best possible steps.

Privacy Consulting & Counter-Surveillance

Do you feel as if you are under constant surveillance from either state institutions, corporations, or someone you may know? Well, you're probably not wrong. We are here to help you with countering said surveillance with privacy-infused devices, phone spyware checks, or even bug sweeping. No tracker in your device, vehicle, home, or office will survive our watchful eye.

Digital Footprint Analysis

As the old saying goes: "the internet never forgets". Unless your operational security is truly top notch, you are bound to have littered the internet with your data at some point, voluntarily or not. We will search the surface web and deep web for your information and provide you with a comprehensive report of what's out there.

Anti-Fraud & Deanonymization Investigations

Have you or anyone close to you ever been the target of a stalker, fraudster, or scammer? It's likelier than you think. These malicious actors tend to hide behind anonymity and fake identities, but where there is a will, there is a way. We will track them down for you and provide you with a report you can submit to the appropriate authorities.

Research & Development

At the end of the day, CzechAlert is not merely an investigative association, but also a research and development-focused institution. We will be regularly publishing papers, analyses, and essays related to the art of investigation, digital rights, security, geopolitics, and more. We are also in the process of developing our own software - more on that coming soon!

Who we are

CzechAlert z. s. was originally conceptualized as a software development company back in 2023, with a focus on user-generated intelligence. Since then, a lot has changed. We have branched out into investigations, privacy consulting, academic research, and digital rights advocacy, with these activities now representing our main focus.

Not only is CzechAlert a passion project, but a vision for the future. Our goal is to create a network of high-level professionals specializing in strategic intelligence, security research, business intelligence, technological innovation, advocacy, open-source intelligence, human intelligence, legislative proposals and much more, who can then advise where and when needed. We consider ethical conduct and client confidentiality to be our top priorities, and we will always stick by our principles.
